## Introduction: Understanding the Numbers Behind Your SBI Clerk Result

Wondering if your score in the SBI Clerk Mains exam is enough for final selection? This uncertainty is common among aspirants waiting for results. The answer lies in the **official cut-off marks**, which define the minimum score required to qualify.

SBI Clerk cut-offs vary widely across **states and reservation categories**, making it essential to understand how they are determined and what trends reveal.

---

## What Exactly Are SBI Clerk Cut-Off Marks?

The **SBI Clerk Cut Off** is the minimum score a candidate must secure to qualify for the next stage or the final merit list.

Cut-offs are released separately for:
- **Preliminary Examination**
- **Main Examination**

> **Important:** There is **no sectional cut-off** in either the SBI Clerk Prelims or Mains exam.
