## What Exactly Are IBPS RRB PO Cut Off Marks?

Cut-off marks are the **minimum scores** required to qualify for the next stage of the selection process.

### Exam Stages and Cut-Offs
- **Preliminary Exam**
- **Main Exam**
- **Interview**

Final merit is prepared using **Mains + Interview scores in an 80:20 ratio**.

### Types of Cut-Offs
1. **Sectional Cut-Off** – Minimum marks in each subject  
2. **Overall Cut-Off** – Minimum total marks  

A candidate must clear **both** to progress.
